How to uprade from SmartContent 1.0 Beta
========================================

In order to upgrade the module, you will need to :

- Upload the complete /smartcontent folder into the /modules directory of your website

- Then, log in to your site as administrator, go to System Admin > Modules, look for the Smartcontent icon in the list of installed modules and click in the update icon

- Click on 'Check Tables' from the SmartContent top menu

That's it !

Enjoy !
